Most Travelled to Place

The place I have visited the most is Fuerteventura. I have been to Fuerteventura 4 times with my family. We visited in 2012 and had no hesitation in coming back.
The main attraction is the main beach. It is ranked as one of the best beaches in the world and is approximately 7 kilometres long. The weather in Fuerteventura is perfect during the summer months, ranging between 22 and 28 degrees.
The main town is stretched along the coast and is home to a number of beautiful restaurants. The locals are very friendly and the views from the coast are in a different class.

Favourite Place

My favourite place to visit is Rome. I was in there in 2015 with my family for a week. I was taken aback by the beautiful architecture all over the city. No matter where I looked there was another impressive building.
We visited all the main attractions like the Colosseum and the Vatican and they were just as good as expected if not better.
Although the temperatures and prices were a bit too high, I hope to visit Rome again some day.

Where I Most Want To Visit

The place I most want to visit is America. I have always been fascinated by America, seeing it so frequently on television. Every state almost seems like a new country and that diversity is intriuging to me.
There are so many places in America that I would love to visit such as New York, Boston and California. I hope to get the chance to undertake my third year work placement in the states and I could also see myself living there in the future.
